How to Play this game

Click 'ROLL DICE' to get your score.

This score will keep adding to your 'CURRENT' score.

If you press hold, this 'CURRENT' score will get added to your main score.

If you keep pressing 'ROLL DICE' and you get '1' on your dice, your 'CURRENT' score will turn '0' and next player will get the chance to roll dice.

'NEW GAME' is to reset the game.
